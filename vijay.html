C. Joseph Vijay (born June 22, 1974) is an Indian politician and former actor who is the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu. He founded the political party Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK) in 2024 and took office as the state's 9th Chief Minister in May 2026, leading a coalition government.Before transitioning into full-time politics, he was one of the highest-paid superstars in Tamil cinema.Political Career: Following his party's stellar debut in the 2026 elections, Vijay secured the Perambur constituency. His rise to power ended nearly six decades of alternating Dravidian rule in Tamil Nadu, taking oath as Chief Minister on May 10, 2026.Acting Career: Known to fans simply as Vijay, he made his cinematic debut as a child artist before starring as a lead in 1992. Over a three-decade career, he won multiple Tamil Nadu State Film Awards and starred in blockbuster hits before stepping away from the film industry.
